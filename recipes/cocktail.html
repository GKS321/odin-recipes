Annex Theater Champagne Cocktail

Description

Three simple ingredients: Champagne, sugar and Angostura bitters add up to an elegant and refreshing sipper.

Ingredients

  • 1 sugar cube
  • 1 dash bitters (such as AngosturaÂ®)

Steps
